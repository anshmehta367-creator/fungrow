Our Story

It's Fun to Grow —
for everyone

Funngro was born from gratitude. Built by educators, tech enthusiasts, IIM alumni, finance experts, and most importantly — by loving parents — we are thankful to the mentors who helped us in the early stages of our lives.

Funngro is our way of giving back to society. Our mission is to enable teenagers to become financially empowered and grow up to be responsible, confident citizens who are ready for the real world.

Today, Funngro is India's largest teen freelancer platform — featured on Shark Tank India, trusted by 4,000+ companies, and home to over 50 lakh talented teenlancers across the country.
